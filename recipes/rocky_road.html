Emma Morris' Rocky Road

16 squares
15 mins
100g butter, cubed 300g chocolate, broken into pieces 3tbsp golden syrup 135g rich tea biscuits, broken into pieces 1 handful mini marshmallows 140g chocolate bars, chopped
Put the butter, chocolate and syrup in a pan over a low heat. Melt them together, stirring until smooth. Cool for about 10 mins. Line a 17cm square tin with foil - or any tin you have about that size. Stir the biscuits and sweets into the chocolate until they are all covered. Pour and spoon the mixture into the tin (don't eat too much as you do it!), and spread the mixture out. Put in the fridge until hard, then cut into squares or fingers.
